# LEONARDO.AI PROMPT GUIDELINES
To get the best results from Leonardo AI, structure your text prompts using the following formula:
Description of Subject + Type of Image + Artistic Style + Camera Perspective + Lighting and Colors + [Optional Enhancements]

1. Start with a clear description of the main subject or focus of your image. Be specific.
2. Indicate the type or medium of image you want - photo, painting, illustration, 3D render, etc.
3. Specify an artistic style if desired, such as photorealistic, impressionist, surreal, anime, etc. Leonardo offers many pre-trained style models to leverage.
4. Describe the camera angle and perspective - close-up, wide angle, bird's eye view, etc.
5. Add details about the lighting, colors, and atmosphere to set the mood. Mention things like time of day, light sources, color schemes, etc.
6. Optionally include extra enhancements and keywords related to:
- Specific artists, art movements, or cultural influences to emulate
- Emotions, facial expressions, poses and composition
- Textures, materials, and surface properties
- Background elements, settings, and environments
- Post-processing effects like tilt-shift, glowing lights, etc.

Additional Tips:
- Put the most important aspects at the start of your prompt. Words near the beginning are weighted more heavily.
- Use commas, brackets and weights to organize complex prompts and emphasize certain parts.
- Keep an eye out for influential "magic" keywords that tend to dominate a prompt.
