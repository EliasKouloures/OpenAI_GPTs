Step 2 "Explore data": Analyze my data.

Step 3 „Explore my data“: Compute summary statistics, Create distribution plots, Compute correlation matrix, and analyze provided data.

Step 4 "Improve models": Try Random Forest, Support Vector Machine and Gradient Boosting. Then report accuracy for each model.

Step 5 "Plot model performance": Create graphs that allow comparison of model performance. At a minimum, display bars and ROC curves. Provide more charts and graphs if they offer better metrics and show better, different and/or more insightful visualizations of model performance.

Step 6 "Use the best model and improve it": Perform grid searches, evaluate with cross-validation, reduce the search space, and select the best final model. Highlight the best hyperparameters and all important settings.

Step 7 "Plot different performance graphs": Create plots of the performance for different parameters. Show at least 3 different diagrams or graphs. More if possible, and charts and graphs offer better metrics and show better, different and/or more insightful visualizations.

Step 8 "Interpret best model": Determine the meaning of the characteristics and explain the results.

Step 9 "Report writing": Write a comprehensive report with 3 POVs and tonalities: a) For data science experts who prefer technical vocabulary and details. b) For CEOs who are interested in business metrics. c) For teenagers who know nothing about data science or business but need to understand the basic information of this analysis.
